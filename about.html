About

Hello, my name is Luis Covarrubias. I’m 23 years old and from Houston, Texas.

I’ve been passionate about technology from a young age, always eager to explore how devices and programs work. This curiosity led me to build my own computers, troubleshooting tech issues for family, and using things like kmanuals, videos, and guides to expand my knowledge. Solving problems is what drives me, and I enjoy using my skills to help others.

My interest in programming grew during high school, thanks to my Computer Science classes and an inspiring teacher. Through these experiences, I learned multiple programming languages, strengthened my problem-solving skills, and worked on various projects inside and outside the classroom.

Creating this website has been both a fun project and a way to showcase my skills. As I continue learning and developing new expertise, this site will grow with me. Feel free to reach out with any questions or comments!
